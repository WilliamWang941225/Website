About

A site describing my learning process.

Hello there, my name is William Wang, also known as Zhao-Guo Wang. I'm a passionate learner who loves to explore physics and programming in my leisure time. Inspired by a senior student in my high school, I was selected to be a member of the National Physics Team in grade 11. Eventually, I won gold medals in both the Asian and the International Physics Olympiad in 2023.
